## 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm an **innovative software engineer with 10+ years of experience** in cloud-native systems, DevOps automation, and AI-driven backend architecture. Former **Cloud Engineer & Map Analyst at Google**, now channeling all of that experience into building **MealOBox** — a tiffin subscription platform connecting students and working professionals with local home-cooked meal vendors across India.

I specialize in building **high-availability distributed systems**, **LLM-integrated services**, and **developer tooling** — with a deep passion for reliability engineering and automation.

- 🍱 **Currently Building:** [MealOBox](https://github.com/mealobox) — Foodtech startup revolutionizing tiffin services in Lucknow
- 🧠 **Currently Learning:** LLM agent architectures, React Native, and seed funding strategy
- 🎙️ **Speaker** at Google I/O Extended and Google DEVFEST events across India
- 🏆 **Mentored 1000+** engineers on DevOps, Cloud, SRE, and career growth
- ⚡ **Fun fact:** I spend ~6 hours every day listening to music while building things
- 📍 Based in **Lucknow, India**
